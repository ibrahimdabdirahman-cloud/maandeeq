04 — Getting here

A few minutes from almost anywhere.

01 / Rail & Elizabeth line

By train

Woolwich Arsenal (National Rail) and Woolwich (Elizabeth line) are a 4-minute walk. Exit toward Powis Street, then turn onto Anglesea Road.

02 / Bus & DLR

By bus or DLR

DLR: Woolwich Arsenal terminus, 5 min walk. Buses: 53, 54, 96, 161, 177, 178, 244, 380, 386, 422, 472, 53 — all stop within 200m.

03 / Car & parking

By car

Paid street parking on Anglesea Road and surrounding streets. Tesco Car Park on Lakedale Road is the easiest if we're full — about 3 minutes on foot.
