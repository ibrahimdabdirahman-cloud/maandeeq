From inquiry to the first scoop of bariis.

4 steps · 7 days

Tell us the date and headcount

Drop us a note with your event date, postcode, and rough headcount. We'll come back within 24 hours with a tailored quote and a draft menu.

≈ 24 hours
Step 02

Shape the menu together

A short call or WhatsApp thread to pick proteins, swap sides, flag allergies, and confirm timing for delivery and warm-up. We'll send a final menu and invoice.

We cook on the day

Lamb goes in at dawn so it falls off the bone by lunch. Sambusas are folded that morning, bariis is finished an hour before we load the van.
